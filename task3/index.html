KOLHAPUR FLOODS DONATION DRIVE

Start Date : 7 August 2021

Kolhapur, a city in Maharashtra, is currently experienced floods due to extreme rains and water logging. People have lost their homes and their belongings. Many are injured and families do not have anything for food or sheter.

Donate even the smallest amount as possible. As we all know that the people affected in the floods are in need of food and shelter and first-aid medicine facilities, these donations will be directly forwarded to these supply chains and the supplies will be provided at the earliest.



About


SAHAKAARYA NGO

SAHAKAARYA is an NGO Foundation based in Mumbai which provides the necessary food and medicinal help to the people in need by conducting food donation camps, medical camps, vaccination drives and much more.
